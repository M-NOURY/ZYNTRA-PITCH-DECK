07 / QASSAS — 3D Interactive Explorer
QASSAS
AUTONOMOUS AI FIELD ROBOT · 3D EXPLORER
Hover over sensors on the 3D model to explore capabilities. Click to lock details.
LiDAR · 3D Mapping
360° rotating LiDAR at 200m range, 300,000 points/sec. Builds centimetre-accurate live point-cloud terrain maps. Powers collision avoidance, autonomous path planning and simultaneous localisation and mapping (SLAM) in GPS-denied environments including underground mine shafts.
pXRF · Chemical Analysis
Mounted on the robotic arm, the portable XRF probe presses against rock surfaces and delivers quantitative elemental analysis in under 3 seconds — detecting Au, Cu, Li, Cr, Ni and 70+ other elements. Replaces traditional sampling workflows that take days and cost $200–500 per sample in lab fees.
Hyperspectral Camera
256-channel visible-to-shortwave-infrared (VNIR-SWIR) spectral camera captures full hyperspectral data cubes as the robot walks. Onboard AI classifies alteration mineralogy, lithological contacts and hydrothermal halos at centimetre resolution — providing the same information as airborne hyperspectral surveys at a fraction of the cost.
RTK-GNSS · Precision GPS
Real-time kinematic GPS with fixed base station correction achieves 1–2 cm horizontal positioning accuracy. Every pXRF reading, every spectral pixel and every LiDAR point is tagged to an exact coordinate — enabling seamless integration into GIS systems, drill-hole planning tools and regulatory submissions.
STC 5G · Live Streaming
STC 5G primary link with Wi-Fi 6 and LEO satellite fallback ensures the QASSAS robot maintains continuous uplink even in remote desert terrain. All sensor data — up to 2 GB/hour — streams live to the ZYNTRA cloud platform for real-time AI processing, remote monitoring and mission control.
12-DOF Legs · Mobility
4 legs × 3 independently actuated joints = 12 DOF. The QASSAS gait controller handles 45° slopes, loose gravel, sand dunes and excavation pit walls. IP68-rated for dust and water. Operates in +55°C ambient temperature with active thermal management. Battery hot-swap in under 5 minutes extends operational range to 30 km/day.
